Type these cheats into the ingame chat box.

forceore = 1000 ore

forcecarbon = 1000 carbon

forcefood = 1000 food

forcesight = remove fog of war

forcebuild = instant build and research

forcenova = 1000 nova

tarkin = All enemies destroyed

skywalker = Mission won

forceboat = press comma to see ship over water

simonsays = Ewok monster(simon the killer ewok)

darkside <1-8> = Destroy enemy number

forceexplore = Show map

Get the Queen

In mission one of the Wookie campaign you can get queen Amidala. All you have to do is go to the east of the level. You should see Tusken Raiders and different buildings. As soon as you get there the buildings will become yours and you will get queen Amidabla. NOTE: I found the easiest way to do this is to first put the "forceexplore" code in, then scroll over to the section and type "simonsays". This way you will have guys there instantly. After you get the queen she can't die or else you fail the mission.
